# Cracked cookie prompt template

Subject: same cookie as closed state, just after crack, states must match

Prompt:

```text
single fortune cookie in cracked transition state, same three-quarter camera angle as the closed cookie, same centered framing, same stylized illustration style, same warm palette and lighting, visible crack seam and early separation, mobile product art, transparent or plain removable background, keep proportions and silhouette consistent across states
```

Negative prompt:

```text
new cookie design, different angle, photoreal tabletop, multiple cookies, extra crumbs, dramatic explosion, hands, text, watermark, background scene, cropped object, inconsistent light direction
```
