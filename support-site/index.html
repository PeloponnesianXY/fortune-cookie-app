Privacy Policy

Private by design.

Fortune Cookie for Your Mood does not collect, store, or share personal data.

The app lets users enter a mood locally on their device and receive a fortune. That information stays on the device and is not transmitted to a server.

No account creation required
No credit card information collected
No analytics or personal profile storage
Mood input remains local to the device
